Course focus

Our IELTS classes combine exam technique with language development. Learners practice real task types, review common band-score problems, receive targeted correction, and get band-score tips.

Writing

Task 1 and Task 2 guidance, structure, grammar accuracy, vocabulary, and timed essays.

Speaking

Speaking practice, fluency, pronunciation, idea development, and direct feedback.

Reading

Reading strategies for faster answers, question analysis, time control, and answer accuracy.

Listening

Listening skills, note-taking, prediction, spelling, distractors, and section-by-section practice.

IELTS support includes

  • Academic and General Training support
  • Mock tests, correction, and band-score tips
  • Physical and online classes
  • Registration support
  • Focused practice for learners targeting a specific band score
